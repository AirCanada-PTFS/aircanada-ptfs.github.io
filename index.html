Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ACA Utilities ("the Bot") collects, uses, stores, and protects information obtained through its operation within Discord servers.

By using ACA Utilities, users acknowledge and agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

User Information

  • Discord User IDs
  • Usernames and Display Names
  • User Mentions
  • User Roles and Permissions

Server Information

  • Server IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Server Configuration Data

Messages and Content

The Bot may process and store message content when necessary for:

  • Command processing
  • Ticket systems
  • Application systems
  • Moderation systems
  • Logging systems
  • Automated responses

Application and Ticket Data

Information submitted through tickets, applications, forms, and feedback systems may be stored for operational and record-keeping purposes.

3. How We Use Information

  • Processing commands and requests
  • Managing support tickets
  • Handling applications
  • Performing moderation actions
  • Maintaining audit logs
  • Managing server settings and permissions
  • Preventing abuse, spam, and malicious activity
  • Improving functionality and reliability

4. Data Storage and Retention

Data may be stored using databases, cloud infrastructure, hosting providers, and other services required to operate the Bot.

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide functionality, maintain moderation records, preserve ticket history, troubleshoot issues, or comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

ACA Utilities does not sell, rent, or trade user data.

Information may only be disclosed when required by law, to comply with legal requests, or to protect the security and integrity of the Bot and its users.

6. Third-Party Services

ACA Utilities may utilize third-party platforms and services, including Discord, hosting providers, database providers, and infrastructure services necessary for operation.

These providers may process information in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms of service.

7. Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. User Rights

Users may request information regarding data associated with their Discord account where technically feasible.

Users may also request deletion of stored data, subject to operational, moderation, security, legal, or record-keeping requirements.

9. Children's Privacy

ACA Utilities is not intended for individuals under the minimum age required to use Discord under applicable laws and Discord's Terms of Service.

10. Discord Compliance

ACA Utilities operates in accordance with Discord's Developer Terms of Service, Developer Policy, and API Terms. The Bot only accesses information necessary to provide its intended functionality.

11. Data Deletion Requests

Users may request deletion of data associated with their Discord account by contacting the ACA Utilities Development Team through the official support server.

Requests will be reviewed and processed where technically feasible and where retention is not required for moderation, security, operational, legal, or record-keeping purposes.

12. Limitation of Liability

ACA Utilities is provided on an "as is" and "as available" basis. The development team makes no guarantees regarding availability, reliability, or uninterrupted service.

13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in functionality, legal requirements, or operational practices. Continued use of the Bot after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

14. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests, please contact the ACA Utilities Development Team through our official support server.
